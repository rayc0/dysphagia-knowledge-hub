# Oral Nutrition Supplements (ONS) Guide for Filipino Patients with Dysphagia

> **TL;DR:** Kapag ang dysphagia patients ay may difficulty sa normal na pagkain, kailangan nila ng **oral nutrition supplements (ONS)** para maiwasan ang malnutrition at dehydration. Ang susi ay pumili ng ONS na tumutugma sa IDDSI level ng patient, regular na sukat ng malnutrition risk gamit ang MNA-SF, at pag-aalaga sa tropical climate dehydration. Ang maraming popular ONS brands ay available sa Philippine pharmacies, pero kung walang PhilHealth coverage, may affordable alternatives.

## Ano Ang Oral Nutrition Supplements (ONS) at Bakit Kailangan Ito

Ang **ONS** ay pre-made na liquid, pudding, o powder nutrition drink na specially formulated para sa mga taong may difficulty kumain. Ito ay may complete nutrients—protein, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, minerals—sa maliliit na volume.

**Bakit important para sa dysphagia patients:**

- **Dysphagia patients ay kumakain ng mas kaunti** — texture-modified meals ay mas maliit ang portion size kaysa regular meals, kaya hindi sapat ang nutrition
- **Thickened foods ay mas mahirap kainin** — thick liquids at pureed foods ay kailangan ng mas maraming pagsisikap, kaya napapagod ang patients
- **Risk ng malnutrition:** 71.6% ng dysphagia patients sa Philippine hospitals ay may malnutrition risk (Bach Mai study adapted sa PH context)
- **Risk ng dehydration:** Thickened drinks ay binabawasan ang fluid intake dahil "heavy" ang feeling, lalo na sa mainit na klima

ONS ay mabilis na paraan na bigyan ng complete nutrition sa maliit na volume.

## Kailan Kailangan Ang Malnutrition Screening: MNA-SF

Bago bumili ng ONS, dapat malaman kung may malnutrition risk ba talaga ang patient. Ang **MNA-SF (Mini Nutritional Assessment—Short Form)** ay simpleng 6-tanong screening tool na ginagamit sa buong mundo para sa elderly.

**Ang 6 tanong ng MNA-SF:**

| Tanong | Sagot Options | Points |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| Recent food intake nadulot ba ng illness/stress? | 0-2 points | — |
| Weight loss sa nakaraang 3 buwan? | 0-3 points | — |
| Mobility—kaya pa bang lumakad? | 0-2 points | — |
| Psychological stress o acute illness? | 0-2 points | — |
| Dementia o depression? | 0-2 points | — |
| Body Mass Index (BMI) | 0-3 points | — |

**Interpretation:**
- **Score ≥ 12** = Normal nutritional status (no ONS needed yet—focus sa regular soft foods)
- **Score 8–11** = At-risk ng malnutrition (need ONS 1–2 times daily between meals)
- **Score < 8** = Malnutrition na (need ONS 2–3 times daily + medical supervision)

**Tanong: Saan makakakuha ng MNA-SF form?**
- Maraming Philippine hospitals ay may MNA-SF sa ER o dietitian office
- Pwede ring i-download online at fillout-in papunta sa dietitian

## IDDSI Levels at ONS Compatibility

Ang **critical requirement para sa ONS ay dapat ito tugma sa IDDSI level** ng patient. Kung may dysphagia ang patient na kailangan ng IDDSI Level 2 (mildly thick), ang ONS ay dapat Level 2 din—otherwise, aspiration risk.

**Common IDDSI levels ng ONS products:**

| IDDSI Level | Viscosity Example | ONS Form | Examples | Safety |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Level 0 (Thin)** | Water thickness | Liquid drink | Abbott Resource Drink, Nestlé Nutri-Plus | ❌ NOT safe para sa dysphagia Level 3–7—aspiration risk |
| **Level 1 (Slightly thick)** | Sippable | Liquid drink | Most standard ONS drinks | ⚠️ Safe lang kung patient kaya Level 1 thin liquids |
| **Level 2 (Mildly thick)** | Flows off spoon | Liquid drink / stirrable powder | Resource Thickened Drink, Nutricia Nutilis | ✅ Safest para sa majority ng dysphagia patients |
| **Level 3 (Moderately thick)** | Cannot sip through straw | Pudding-like | Fresubin Crème, Nestlé Nutri Pudding | ✅ Para sa IDDSI Level 3–4 patients |
| **Level 4 (Pureed)** | Soft mound on fork | Pudding, yogurt-like | Resource Pudding | ✅ Para sa IDDSI Level 4–5 patients |

**Critical safety rule:** Kung uncertain ang IDDSI level ng patient, palaging mas-thick ang ONS—better to start thick at i-thin down, hindi vice versa.

## Paano Masiguro Ang IDDSI Level Ng ONS

**Problem:** Karamihan ng ONS products sa Pilipinas ay WALANG IDDSI labeling. Paano alam kung safe ba?

**Solusyon: Do-It-Yourself IDDSI Flow Test sa bahay**

Kung may 10ml syringe (available sa pharmacies para sa ₱15–25):
1. Fill syringe with ONS
2. Hold vertically (tip down, plunger up)
3. Remove finger from plunger, let it fall naturally
4. After 10 seconds, measure remaining liquid:
   - **< 1ml remaining** = IDDSI Level 0 (thin)
   - **1–4ml remaining** = IDDSI Level 1 (slightly thick)
   - **4–8ml remaining** = IDDSI Level 2 (mildly thick)
   - **> 8ml remaining** = IDDSI Level 3 (moderately thick)

**Easy trick:** Kung ang ONS ay "pours like ketchup" = probably Level 2 (safe para sa most dysphagia patients).

## MNA-SF Assessment + ONS Recommendations Table

| MNA-SF Score | Nutritional Status | ONS Recommendation | Frequency | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ≥ 12 | Normal | None required; focus on soft foods | — | Prevent malnutrition |
| 8–11 | At-risk | 1–2 cups ONS daily | Between meals (mid-morning, 3pm) | Stop weight loss |
| < 8 | Malnourished | 2–3 cups ONS daily | Between meals + after meals | Gain weight, improve strength |

**Important:** Ang ONS ay **supplement, hindi replacement** para sa regular meals. ONS ay dapat idagdag sa normal na pagkain, hindi palitan ito.

## PhilHealth Coverage: Current Status at Alternatives

**Current PhilHealth Coverage (2024–2025):**
- ❌ **ONS for elderly dysphagia patients ay HINDI covered** ng regular PhilHealth benefit package
- ✅ PhilHealth nag-cover ng **severe acute malnutrition sa children under 5** (October 2024 bagong benefit package)
- ✅ **Senior citizens ay automatic PhilHealth coverage**, pero para sa consultation lang, hindi ONS products

**Alternatives kung walang budget:**
1. **Government hospital dietitian referral** — free nutritional counseling kung paano gumawa ng homemade high-protein soft meals
2. **SMS (Senior Massage Services) / community health center** — may libreng nutrition education para sa seniors
3. **DepEd school feeding programs** — kung may living-in na grandchild na may access
4. **Social welfare offices** — may emergency assistance fund para sa seniors na indigent

**Tignan:** Maraming provincial hospitals ay may partnership with Nestlé o Abbott para sa discounted ONS para sa indigent patients—tanungin ang hospital social worker.

## Dehydration Prevention sa Tropical Climate Philippines

Ang **dysphagia + thickened fluids + tropical heat = triple dehydration risk** sa Pilipinas.

**Bakit malaking problema sa PH:**
- Average temperature sa Pilipinas = 26–32°C (higher than most temperate countries)
- Elderly na may reduced thirst sensation ay naturally nag-iiwan ng pag-inom
- Thickened drinks ay parang "heavy" — patients naturally inumin ng mas kaunti
- Result: 30–40% ng dysphagia patients ay dehydrated

**Signs ng dehydration na dapat i-monitor:**
- Dark/concentrated urine
- Dry mouth / cracked lips
- Constipation (common first sign)
- Dizziness kapag umakyat
- Sunken eyes
- Confusion o lethargy
- Low blood pressure

**Dehydration Prevention Strategy:**

| Time | Fluid Option | Amount | IDDSI Level | Notes |
|------|------|--------|------------|-------|
| **Morning** | Thickened water / Level 2 drink | 200ml (1 cup) | L2 | Use thickener sachet |
| **Mid-morning** | ONS (Nutri-Plus / Ensure) | 200ml | L1–L2 | Counts toward fluid intake |
| **Lunch** | Thickened broth / soup | 150ml | L3–L4 | Hot soup helps with hydration psychologically |
| **Afternoon** | Level 2 water / diluted mango juice | 150ml | L2 | Add thickener if needed |
| **Dinner** | Soft rice with broth | 100–150ml broth | L3–L4 | Absorbed through food |
| **Evening** | Thickened yogurt drink | 150ml | L2–L3 | Coolness helps in hot weather |

**Target:** Minimum 1.5–2 liters per day kahit may dysphagia.

**Pro tip para sa tropical climate:** Keep thickened drinks sa **refrigerator**, hindi sa room temperature. Cold drinks ay mas acceptable sa mainit na panahon.

## Common Mistakes sa ONS Use

1. **Wrong IDDSI level** — Buying ONS na thin (Level 0) para sa IDDSI Level 4 patient = aspiration risk ❌
2. **Using ONS as meal replacement** — Dapat supplement lang, hindi meal replacement ❌
3. **Exceeding caloric needs** — ONS has 200–300 kcal per 200ml—kalkulahin kung hindi overfeed ❌
4. **Ignoring expiration dates** — ONS ay mabilis mabulok sa tropical heat; check dates weekly ✅
5. **Forgetting to stir thickener** — Powder thickeners ay kailangan fully dissolved; stirring for 2–3 minutes ✅
6. **Not monitoring MNA-SF regularly** — Check every month kung improving ba ang nutritional status ✅

## When to Refer to Hospital Dietitian

Pumunta sa hospital dietitian kung:
- MNA-SF score ay hindi umabot ng 12 after 2–3 weeks ng ONS
- Patient ay patuloy na bumababa ang weight
- Nag-choke o nag-cough ang patient habang umiinom ng ONS (IDDSI level ay masyado lumipat)
- Constipation ay persistent kahit may fiber supplements
- Patient ay nag-develop ng rashes o allergy symptoms (lactose intolerance o food allergy)
