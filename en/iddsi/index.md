# IDDSI Framework Guide Collection

The International Dysphagia Diet Standardisation Initiative (IDDSI) provides the globally recognised 8-level framework for classifying foods and drinks for people with dysphagia. This section covers every level in depth — from thin liquids (Level 0) to regular diet (Level 7) — with clinical applications, home testing methods, and practical meal preparation guidance.
