# Dysphagia Diet Standards Guide Collection

Multiple national and regional dysphagia diet classification systems exist globally — IDDSI (international), JSDR (Japan), UDF (Japan), NDD (Australia, pre-IDDSI), and the Greater Bay Area T/SATA standards. This section provides crosswalk guides and practical implementation resources.
