# Dysphagia by Condition: Guide Collection

Dysphagia management varies significantly by underlying condition — the feeding challenges of ALS differ from those of Parkinson's, dementia, or stroke. This section organises guides by diagnosis to help patients, families, and clinicians find condition-specific management strategies.
