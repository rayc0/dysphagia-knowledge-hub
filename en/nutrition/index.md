# Dysphagia Nutrition Guide Collection

Malnutrition and dehydration are the most common complications of dysphagia. This section provides evidence-based guidance on maintaining adequate nutrition within texture-modified diet constraints — covering thickener selection, hydration protocols, protein optimization, oral nutritional supplements, and clinical malnutrition screening tools.
